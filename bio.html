Bio

Environmental engineer and scientist
I received my BSE in Civil Engineering from the University of Iowa in 2021. Currently, I am a PhD candidate in Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Iowa, advised by Dr. Keri Hornbuckle. I study a class of chemicals called polychlorinated biphenyls (PCBs). PCBs are toxic, man-made chemicals that were used in a variety of industrial and commercial applications such as transformers and capacitors, heat transfer and hydraulic fluid, plasticizers, adhesives, and building materials. PCBs were banned in 1979 because of their toxicity; they are carcinogens, endocrine disruptors, and neurotoxins.
