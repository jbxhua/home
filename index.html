Home

Hello, welcome to my website. My name is Jason and I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Iowa, advised by Dr. Keri Hornbuckle as part of the Iowa Superfund Research Program (ISRP). I graduated with a B.S.E. in Civil Engineering at the University of Iowa in 2021 with a focus in Environmental Engineering. Currently, my research focuses on identyifying sources of polychlorinated bihpenyls (PCBs) in schools to improve indoor air quality for staff and students.
