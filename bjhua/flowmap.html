Flowmapping



Places that attract (receive) the most flows, and the places that send the most flows

Places that receive the most flows are generally the largest cities, as expected. These are major cities like Shanghai, Beijing, Shenzhen etc. Beijing seems to have the highest flows in and out because it is located right next to two other major cities. Most of the movement is concentrated in the eastern side of the country where the largest cities are located. This is expected as most of the population is located in the east-central to eastern part of the country. There are also larger flows along the eastern coast compared to the west and central parts of the country. Interestingly, the flows between the western side of the country and eastern side are very low. The western side is also mostly inhabited by ethnic minority groups such as Tibetans and Uyghur.

General direction of flows (e.g., north-south, east-west, etc.) with context to the geography of the flow data set

Generally, movement follows a north-south direction. There is little flow from the western side of the country to the eastern side. This is likely because of large differences in population densities and terrain between the western and eastern side.
